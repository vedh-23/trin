Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the Age Limit to join Trinetra?

We try to recruit young volunteers as the work of a TRI officer is exhausting and requires a good amount of physical and mental strength. Hence, the age limit is between 21-35 years only.

Q2: What is the process of recruitment in the TRI?

Candidates should fill out the form given on the home page. If satisfied with the form, the agency will try to contact you. Then, some physical and mental exams are conducted to filter the applicants. Those who pass are sent to the training center, where again many are filtered due to the tough training course. Those who manage to complete the training become TRI officers.

Q3: What are the prerequisites required to join the service?

Prerequisites required are:

  1. Graduation required
  2. Strong Mental Health
  3. Patriotism
  4. Strong Reason to Join
